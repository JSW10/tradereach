FAQ

Questions tradespeople ask us

Do I need any marketing knowledge?
None at all. Your only job is to send us a voice note, a text, or a photo via WhatsApp when you finish a job. We handle every word of writing, every piece of strategy, and every publish. You never need to log in anywhere or understand how any of it works.
How quickly will I start getting more leads?
Social media and your Google Business Profile start building trust and visibility from day one. SEO blog posts typically take 3–6 months to rank on Google — but once they do, they keep working indefinitely. The longer you're on TradeReach, the stronger your position gets.
What trades do you work with?
Every UK trade — builders, plumbers, electricians, roofers, landscapers, decorators, joiners, kitchen and bathroom fitters, groundworkers, and more. If you work in the trades and want more customers finding you online, we can help.
Do I need a website to get started?
No. Our Social plan only needs your Facebook and Instagram pages. For SEO blogs you'll need a website — but we can build one for you for £299 setup + £29/mo, or add it to any plan as an extra.
Is there a contract or lock-in?
No contracts, ever. You pay month-to-month and can cancel with 30 days notice. We earn your business every single month by delivering results — we don't need to lock you in.
How much time does it actually take me?
Typically 2–3 minutes per job you want to feature — a voice note, a text, or a photo via WhatsApp. Send one when you finish a job and we'll handle everything else. Even one update per month gives us enough to work with, though more is better.
